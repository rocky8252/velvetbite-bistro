Welcome to L'Olivier

Where Passion Meets Culinary Artistry

Located in the heart of the city, L'Olivier is a celebration of classic Southern French and coastal Italian cuisine. Founded by family chefs, our kitchen honors ancient slow-cooking traditions, high-quality wood fires, and vibrant herbs.

Farm-To-Table Fresh

All greens, cheese toppings, and extra-virgin olive oils are imported from organic farming cooperatives.

Traditional Fire Hearth

Our doughs and steaks are cooked over seasoned oak and grapevines to infuse a rich smoky note.
