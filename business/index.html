Help & Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

How does direct WhatsApp ordering work without aggregator commissions?
When you order via our digital menu, your dish choices and table details are pre-formatted into a direct WhatsApp message sent to our desk manager. You pay 0% commission fees and enjoy direct kitchen service!
Can I reserve specific dining zones for birthday or corporate events?
Yes! Our Table Booking system lets you select Garden Terrace, Matcha Lounge, Espresso Bar, or Private VIP Lounge for groups over 8 guests.
Do you cater to vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free diets?
Absolutely. All menu items are tagged Veg, Vegan, or Non-Veg with calorie counts and preparation times. Guests can filter by dietary preference.
What are the kitchen operating hours?
Our kitchen operates daily 11:30 AM to 11:30 PM. Happy hour specials run 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM Monday through Friday.
Do you offer corporate catering or wedding bulk orders?
Yes, full catering for corporate lunches, weddings, and private events. Contact us via WhatsApp or the email form below for a custom quote within 24 hours.
How does the QR code digital menu work at my table?
Every table has a branded QR standee. Point your phone camera at it — our full digital menu opens in your browser. No app needed. Search, filter, and order directly via WhatsApp.
