Help & Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

How does direct WhatsApp ordering work without aggregator commissions?
When you order via our digital menu, your dish choices, quantities, and table/delivery details are pre-formatted into a direct WhatsApp message sent to our desk manager. You pay 0% extra commission fees and enjoy direct kitchen service!
Can I reserve specific dining zones for private birthday or corporate events?
Yes! Our Table Booking system allows you to select between the Garden Terrace (Outdoor), Matcha Lounge (Cozy Tatami), and Espresso Bar. For group parties over 8 guests, select 'Private VIP Lounge'.
Do you cater to vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free dietary requirements?
Absolutely. All menu items are tagged with 🟢 Veg, 🌿 Vegan, 🥩 Non-Veg markers, along with calorie tags and preparation times so guests can filter items by dietary preferences.
What are the kitchen service operating hours for table bookings?
Our kitchen operates daily from 11:30 AM to 11:30 PM. Happy hour specials are valid between 04:00 PM and 07:00 PM Monday through Friday.
Do you offer catering or bulk orders for corporate offices and weddings?
Yes, we offer full catering services for corporate lunches, wedding receptions, and private events. Contact us via WhatsApp or the email form below to receive a customized quote within 24 hours.
How do I scan the QR code digital menu at my table?
Every table has a branded QR standee. Open your phone camera, point at the QR code, and it opens our full digital menu in your browser — no app installation needed. You can filter by category, search items, and order directly via WhatsApp.
